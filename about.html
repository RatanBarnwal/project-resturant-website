About Us

Desi Tadka is a pure vegetarian restaurant which serves different cuisines, prepared under the supervision of Chef Vishal Yadav (awarded former chef from Oberoi group of hotels and resorts).The food is prepared with high quality of ingredients, fresh vegetables, passion and love. The aim of the restaurant is to provide homestyle food at economical prices. With the support and guidance of Mrs. Sikha Yadav, founder of Desi Tadka, Chef Vishal Yadav started this restaurant in 2016 as a take away outlet. Within a year, the restaurant has become very popular in the area because of the homely taste of the food and their passion towards cooking and hospitality.

Expect the best

Desi Tadka restaurant is also famous for its signature dishes which are very hard to find in the restaurants nowadays.Litti chokha,Dahi Batasa ,Mixed Paratha, Veg Kawab, and many more are served at Desi Tadka. Our food expert Mrs. Sikha Yadav, wife of Mr. Vishal Yadav, prepared these dishes with her secret spices and experience. The primary aim of the restaurant is to provide the best and hygienic food to all our customers. Nowadays people are very busy in their work life and don’t have enough time to cook for themselves, so it is very important to have a nutritious and hygienic meal. For those, Desi Tadka is a heaven as people get the nutritious food as well as great hospitality

Only for foodies

Desi Tadka is a pure vegetarian family restaurant managed by Chef Vishal Yadav. The restaurant serves varieties of cuisines like Indian, Chinese, Tandoori and South Indian. Our Head Chef ensures that the food is prepared hygienically and retains its natural flavours and taste. Started in 2016 as a takeaway unit, Desi Tadka has now become a fine dining restaurant with 100 covers. The old village theme of the restaurant and homely touch in the food makes people to come and celebrate their evenings and special occasions in the restaurant.
